About Us

Welcome to the Student Utility Program! This comprehensive tool is designed to assist students with various academic calculations and utilities.

Created by Tanishk Vashistha, this program offers a user-friendly interface for:

  • Percentage calculations
  • CGPA calculations
  • Fibonacci sequence generation
  • Factorial calculations
  • Unit conversions
  • Physics calculations
  • Number tools (prime checking, even/odd, reverse)

Our goal is to make academic life easier and more efficient for students worldwide.
